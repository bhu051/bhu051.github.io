WELCOME TO ENI FOOTBALL ACADEMY

We are excited to have you here. At Eni Football Academy, we’re more than just a football school we’re a family built on passion, discipline, and dreams. Whether you're taking your first kick or training for greatness, this is the place where your journey begins. Our goal is to develop strong, confident players on and off the pitch. With expert coaching, a supportive environment, and a vision for excellence, you’re in the right place to grow, compete, and shine. Thank you for joining us. Let’s build the future of football together!




We offer holiday camps, position-focused workshops (e.g., strikers, goalkeepers), and leadership clinics to build complete players physically and mentally.



Training Levels
Beginner: Learn the basics in a fun and safe environment.
Intermediate: Sharpen your skills and football IQ.
Advanced: Master-level training for elite competition.
